MOGO - best logbook loans terms

Logbook loans are loans secured using your vehicle's logbook. You are able
to get from 100,000 to 1,500,000 KES within 24 hours and retain the right to
continue driving the car.

model. You can easily repay a loan in affordable installments as you are able
to choose a period of up to 5 years. Your repayment schedule
can be on a weekly or monthly basis depending on your choice.

Our quick logbook loans for old or new cars are easy to secure and are
usually based on a flexible review of your credit history. Apply now online or
at one of our branches in Nairobi, Mombasa, Nakuru, Eldoret,
Kisumu.

Mogo Auto LTD (physical location at Prestige Plaza, Ngong road, Nairobi) is part of the Mogo Finance group, founded in 2012. MOGO group specializes in consumer finance for used cars. Our offices operate in Europe, Asia & Africa. The rapid development of MOGO is possible by the presence of stable equity capital, high-performance technological solutions and effective management of the loan portfolio. In Kenya, MOGO provides financing to passenger cars, logbook loans & boda financing. Loan amounts ranges from 100,000 to 1,500,000 Kenyan Shillings for a period of 6 to 60 months.
